雞蛋豆腐(Egg Tofu)

敘述 Description

自己做雞蛋豆腐,健康無負擔

A simple recipe using just a few ingredients to make clean and wholesome egg tofu.

Chill it in the fridge for a refreshing treat on a hot day!

成份 Ingredients

  • 雞蛋 2顆
    2 eggs
  • 無堂豆漿 190 毫升
    190ml unsweetened soy milk

步驟 Steps

  1. 兩顆雞蛋，無糖豆漿190cc，拌勻過篩
    Beat the eggs and mix with unsweetened soy milk. Strain the mixture, then steam it in a rice cooker.
  2. 電鍋蒸，外鍋水杯8分，鍋蓋插一根筷子留縫蓋上蓋子，跳起來即可
    Add 0.8 cups of water to the outer pot. Place a chopstick between the lid and the pot to leave a small gap. Steam until the switch pops up, and it’s ready.
