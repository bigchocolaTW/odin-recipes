番茄蛋炒飯(Tomato and Egg Fried Rice)

敘述 Description

最喜歡有點溼潤但又粒粒分明的番茄蛋炒飯,除了番茄酸甜香氣外,炒蛋跟鍋氣的香味也很重要！很適合夏天的一道料理～

本食譜為素食(五辛素) 兩人份

I really like Tomato and Egg Fried Rice that’s slightly moist but still has every grain separate.

Besides the sweet and tangy tomato flavor, the taste of scrambled eggs and the wok hei are just as important.It’s a perfect dish for summer.

This is a vegetarian recipe for two servings.

成份 Ingredients

  • 牛番茄 1顆 (1 beef tomato)
  • 洋蔥 1/4顆 (1/4 onion)
  • 白飯 2碗 (2 bowls of cooked rice)
  • 水 少許 (A splash of water)
  • 青蔥 2把 (2 stalks of green onion)
  • 蛋 2顆 (2 eggs)
  • 黑胡椒 少許 (A pinch of black pepper)
  • 番茄醬 2大匙 (2 tablespoons of ketchup)

步驟 Steps

  1. 將所需的材料先準備好，蛋先炒過放旁邊
    Prepare all the ingredients. Scramble the eggs first and set them aside.
  2. 洋蔥與青蔥加少許油拌炒。
    Sauté the onion and green onion with a little oil.
  3. 加入牛番茄拌炒，加少許水，稍燜鍋。
    Add the tomato and stir-fry. Add a splash of water and briefly cover to simmer.
  4. 加入白飯及2大匙番茄醬一起拌炒，可以試味道，看個人口味灑點黑胡椒。
    Add the cooked rice and 2 tablespoons of ketchup. Stir-fry together. Taste and adjust with a pinch of black pepper if desired.
  5. 番茄蛋炒飯完成。
    Tomato and Egg Fried Rice is ready to serve!
