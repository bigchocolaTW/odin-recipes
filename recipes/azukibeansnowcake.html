紅豆雪花糕(Azuki Bean Snow Cake)

敘述 Description

紅豆加上牛奶,甜蜜蜜的幸福感,非常簡單又好吃的一道料理

放冰箱冷藏後非常消暑哦

Sweet azuki beans combined with milk bring a soft and comforting sweetness — simple, delicious, and heartwarming.

Chill it in the fridge for a refreshing treat on a hot day!

成份 Ingredients

  • 市售蜜紅豆 150克
    150g Sweetened Azuki Beans
  • 玉米粉 65克
    65g corn flour
  • 牛奶 500克
    500g milk
  • 砂糖 30g
    30g sugar

步驟 Steps

  1. 將牛奶、砂糖加入鋼盆中拌勻，玉米粉過篩，分2-3次加入，最後拌入蜜紅豆。
    In a mixing bowl, combine the milk and sugar. Sift in the cornstarch in 2 to 3 additions, stirring well each time. Finally, fold in the sweetened azuki beans.
  2. 開小火，邊煮邊攪拌。
    Cook over low heat, stirring continuously.
  3. 大概2-3分鐘後，麵糊會逐漸變濃稠。
    After about 2–3 minutes, the mixture will begin to thicken.
  4. 攪拌至黏稠時就要注意火力不要大，越小越好，持續攪拌呈漿糊狀就可以關火。
    Once it turns sticky and paste-like, lower the heat as much as possible to avoid burning. Keep stirring until it reaches a smooth, pudding-like consistency, then turn off the heat.
